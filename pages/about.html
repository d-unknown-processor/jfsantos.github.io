About me

My name is João Felipe Santos. I received my bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering from the Federal University of Santa Catarina (Florianópolis/Santa Catarina/Brazil) in 2011 and joined the INRS (Montréal/QC/Canada) as a master student in 2012. My main research areas is speech/audio signal processing.

This blog is an attempt to document interesting things such as tools, software libraries, methodologies, etc., that I discovered during my research and (hopefully) can be useful for somebody else.
